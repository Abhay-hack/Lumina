FAQs

Lumina is a platform that connects aspiring professionals with exciting internship and job opportunities. We aim to bridge the gap between talented individuals and companies looking for fresh perspectives.

To apply for an internship or job, visit the ‘Open Positions’ section of our website. Browse through the available opportunities and click ‘Apply’ on the role you're interested in. You'll need to submit your resume and cover letter for consideration.

No prior experience is necessary for most internships, as they are designed to help you gain experience. However, some roles may have specific requirements, which will be listed in the job description.

The application process can take anywhere from 1 to 3 weeks, depending on the role and the number of applicants. Once you apply, our team will review your application, and if you're shortlisted, we'll contact you for an interview.

Yes, you can apply for multiple positions that interest you. Just make sure to tailor your application materials to each specific role to highlight your relevant skills and experience.

After submitting your application, our recruitment team will review it. If your skills and experience match the role, you will be contacted for the next steps, which may include an interview or skills assessment.

Each role has a different application deadline, which will be listed in the job description. Be sure to submit your application before the deadline to be considered.

If you are not selected for the role you applied for, don't be discouraged. We regularly post new opportunities, so keep an eye on our website for other roles that may be a better fit for your skills and interests.

If you have more questions, you can reach us via email at careers@lumina.com or visit the Contact Us page to submit an inquiry. Our team will get back to you as soon as possible.
